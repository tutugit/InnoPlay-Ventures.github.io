About Us

InnoPlay Ventures was established in December 2023 as a venture capital firm specializing in the web3 gaming sector. The team currently consists of 11 members with extensive experience in game development and venture capital.

The gaming industry has witnessed remarkable growth since the 1970s, giving rise to numerous outstanding gaming companies and products that have fundamentally transformed human leisure and entertainment. Concurrently, with the rapid advancement of blockchain technology and the continuous improvement of infrastructure, decentralized gaming is on the verge of widespread implementation, promising players a completely new gaming experience. Humanity is standing on the eve of a massive explosion in web3 gaming.


Against this backdrop, InnoPlay Ventures was founded with the collaborative support of KOEI TECMO HOLDINGS CO., LTD., CITIZEN HOLDINGS CO., LTD., and NOMURA GROUP, three prominent entities contributing to the realization of decentralized gaming.


InnoPlay Ventures is dedicated to early-stage investments in outstanding products within the web3 gaming and infrastructure sectors. We provide comprehensive technical support in areas such as game product development, security audits, and other technological domains. We are committed to relentless efforts in shaping the innovative and promising landscape of the web3 gaming industry.

Sota Kimikawa

Founder & CEO

In 2009, Sota Kimikawa joined the renowned Japanese game development company KOEI TECMO HOLDINGS CO., LTD. (Japanese: 株式会社コーエーテクモホールディングス).
